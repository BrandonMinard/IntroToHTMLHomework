Instructions on how to make a sandwich

  1. Acquire bread
  2. Acquire ingredients, including but not limited to, lunch meat, lettuce, tomato, mustard, mayonaise
  3. S0et two slices of bread out on a plate, side by side so they do not overlap
  4. Laqeur one peice of bread with mustard, and the other with mayo, or any condiment of your choice
  5. Pile a reasonable amount of meat on either slice
  6. Cut the tomato into quarter inch thick slices
  7. Pile a reasonable amount of tomato onto the meat
  8. Pile a reasonable amount of lettuce ontop of the tomato
  9. Place the non-meated and vegetabled bread on top of the other, with the condiment side facing down
  10. Consume your simple sandwich
